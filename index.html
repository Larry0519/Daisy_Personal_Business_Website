Our Company

GW Technology

GW Technology implements web 3.0 technologies in gaming and digital assets for the community. Our main business is blockchain games transformation and NFT services. The GW SDKs provide convenient access to the GW APIs , Smart Contract methods for applications, assisting games studios in establishing a mature economic and education system. For NFT services, we provide consultation and assessment on client project and cost-effective solutions for NFT minting. We aim to be the Steam for blockchain mobile games.

Game Wonderlab

Game Wonderlab is a comprehensive blockchain mobile gaming platform with thousands of blockchain-powered mobile games and hundreds of game development studio partners. The vision of Game Wonderlab is to become a compatible platform for both IOS and Android mobile system and "make sure all the Web 3.0 mobile games are in Game Wonderlab. Be Steam for mobile blockchain games.
